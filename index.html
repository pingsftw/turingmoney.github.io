Turing is Community

Welcome to the Turing project! We are a community of people who support the open internet, and this project is a currency designed to support our cause. When you buy Turing you increase the value of the currency already in the hands of the projects working to help the net, and you can vote on which projects get funding priority as new Turing is issued.
